SAMUTHIRAKANI

Samuthirakani[1] (born 26 April 1973) is an Indian actor and film director who works predominantly in Tamil and Telugu films besides few Malayalam films. He worked as an assistant to director K. Balachander, in Paarthale Paravasam. He won the National Film Award for Best Supporting Actor in 2016 for Visaranai,[2][3] he is also an recipient of three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, a Filmfare Award and a SIIM Aaward. Samuthirakani was born on 26 April 1960 in Seithur, Rajapalayam, Tamil Nadu.[1][4] He belongs to a Telugu family. He did his B.Sc. in Mathematics from the Rajapalayam Rajus College and he also earned a Bachelor of Law from Ambedkar Law College.
