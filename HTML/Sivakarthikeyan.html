SIVAKARTHIKEYAN

Sivakarthikeyan (born 17 February 1985[1]), also known by the initialism SK, is an Indian actor, playback singer, lyricist, film producer and television presenter, who worked predominantly in Tamil films. He was a recipient of a Tamil Nadu State Film Award, four Edison Awards, four SIIMA Awards, and two other Vijay Awards.Sivakarthikeyan married Aarthi on 27 August 2010.[2] The couple have three children: a daughter and two sons.[3][4] Aaradhana sang parts of the song "Vayadi Petha Pulla" from Kanaa alongside her father.[5]
